Data Warehouse

According to Imnon, a Data Warehouse is a

  • Subject oriented - data are organized around sales, products, etc.
  • Integrated - data from multiple sources are combined
  • Time-variant - data is stored to provide historical perspective
  • Non-volatile - data are usually not updated

collection of data

  • usually used to improve decision making
  • single point of truth (SPOT)
